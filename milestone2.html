HISTORY OF HTML

Tim Berners-Lee, the scientist and academic, was the primary author of HTML, with the assistance of his colleagues atCERN, an international scientific oraganization based in geneva. Berners-Lee invented the World Wide Web in 1989 at CERN. The most widely used version throughout the 2000's was HTML 4.01,which became an official standard in December.
